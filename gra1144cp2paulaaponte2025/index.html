Breakfast In a Mug

Mug breakfast recipes are perfect for college students who don’t want to wash a lot of dishes.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Eggs
  • Green onion
  • Diced Tomatos
  • Ham cut into small cubes
  • 1 TSP of milk
  • Any kind of shreded cheese

Steps:

  1. Greese mug with spray butter
  2. Mix all ingredients well
  3. Pour ingredients in the mug
  4. Place mug with ingredients in microwave for 2 min
  5. Make sure the bottom is cooked well
  6. If the eggs are still runny at the bottom, place it again in the microwave for 20 seconds
  7. Enjoy your delicious eggs :)
