Breakfast In a Mug

Mug breakfasts are perfect for lazy students who don't want to wash a lot of dishes.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Green onion
  • Diced Tomatos
  • Ham cut into small pieces
  • 1 TSP of milk
  • Any kind of shreded cheese

Steps

  1. Mix ingredients well
  2. Turn on Microwave for 2 min
  3. Make sue the bottom is not to liquid
  4. If it is liquid, Place it again in the microwave for 20 seconds
